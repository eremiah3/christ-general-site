About Grace Fellowship

We are a Christ-centered community committed to teaching Scripture, worshiping God, and serving our neighbors. Whether you join online or locally, you'll find authentic relationships, sound biblical teaching, and practical ways to live out your faith.

  • Scripture-led — faithful Bible teaching for real life.
  • Prayerful — an active prayer ministry serving needs near and far.
  • Missional — community projects and outreach.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To glorify God by worshiping passionately, growing in Scripture, and serving our neighbors in love and truth.

Vision

A Christ-centered community where people find hope, build strong faith, and impact the world through the Gospel.

Values

Faith • Love • Service — shaping our worship, discipleship, and outreach.

Who We Are

Grace Fellowship is a family of believers committed to walking with Christ and with one another. Expect warm community, Bible-based teaching, active prayer, and practical care.

  • Small groups to grow deeper in Scripture
  • Opportunities to serve and lead
  • Care + prayer for people and families

Living out Worship • Grow • Serve—locally and online.
