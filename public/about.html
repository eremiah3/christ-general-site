Our Mission & Vision

Mission

Witnessing JESUS, Interceding for The Body of Christ and Raising a Generation of Lovers of God.

Vision

JESUS must be Seen. JESUS must be Known. JESUS must be Glorified.

Values

Faith • Love • Service — shaping our worship, discipleship, and outreach.

Who We Are

CHRIST GENERALS is a Family of Lovers of God. We are a nondenominational gathering of Believers. We have given ourselves to Prayer and to the Ministry of the Word..

  • Small groups to grow deeper in Scripture
  • Opportunities to serve and lead
  • Care + prayer for people and families

Our Journey

July 18, 2021

Christ Generals began as a small prayer group by the inspiration of the Holy Spirit upon the Setman, Ayomide Ajibodu.

2021–2022

The Lord brooded over us and people began to join this mighty move of the Spirit, leveraging the online space.

2022

We held our first annual physical prayer conference — EMERGENCE OF GENERALS (EOG).

2023

The Lord instructed us to begin Secondary Schools Evangelism—taking the Gospel of Jesus to young hearts and showing them the hope that shines from the Truth.

2024

The Lord led us to begin Street Evangelism. In December 2024 we hosted a JESUS RALLY in Ajah, Lagos—accompanied by testimonies to the saving power of Jesus.

2025

We held our first Crusade to the glory of our Lord Jesus—three days of intense evangelism by the power of the Holy Spirit.
